A high-performance latent video diffusion transformer implementation written in JAX.

This project implements a state-of-the-art latent video diffusion model, leveraging the power of **JAX** for high-performance training and sampling. By operating in the latent space, the model efficiently generates high-quality video sequences while maintaining manageable computational requirements.

### Technical Highlights
- **Transformer-based Architecture**: Utilizes a diffusion transformer (DiT) backbone for scalable video generation.
- **JAX Implementation**: Written from the ground up in JAX to take advantage of XLA compilation and efficient TPU/GPU utilization.
- **Latent Space Modeling**: Processes compressed representations of video to focus on semantic content rather than pixel-level redundancy.

> The source code is available on GitHub [here](https://github.com/simonryu328/latent-video-diffusion).
